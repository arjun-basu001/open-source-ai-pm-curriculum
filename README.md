A practical, no-fluff curriculum for people building at the intersection of **AI + product**.

If you want to move from “I understand AI concepts” to “I can scope, build, evaluate, and ship AI products responsibly,” this repo is for you.

---

## 🌟 Why this repo exists

Most AI learning paths age quickly or stay too theoretical.
This one is built around:

- durable concepts,
- real production decisions,
- and portfolio-grade outputs.

Each module includes:
- curated resources,
- hands-on exercises,
- and clear exit criteria.

---

## 👥 Who this is for

- **Product Managers** moving into AI-native roles
- **Engineers/Builders** who want stronger product judgment
- **Founders & hybrids** who need both technical and product fluency

---

## ⚡ Quick start

1. Fork this repository
2. Start with **[01 — Foundations](./curriculum/01-foundations.md)**
3. Complete exercises (don’t skip them)
4. Publish artifacts as you go
5. Finish with a portfolio that proves real capability

> Recommended pace: **9–18 weeks** depending on depth and schedule.

## 🧪 Capstone outputs

By the end, you should have three high-signal artifacts:

1. **AI Feature Strategy Brief** (problem, approach, metrics, risk)
2. **Working Prototype** (public URL + repo + eval summary)
3. **Evaluation + Governance Packet** (test cases, taxonomy, risk register)

These three artifacts are your proof of work.
